About H2C 1.0

Hack to Crack 1.0 marked our inaugural hackathon, bringing together brilliant minds from across the nation. The event focused on fostering innovation and creating real-world solutions through technology.

Over 300+ participants from various colleges and universities came together for this 48-hour coding marathon. The event was held at IIIT Bhopal, featuring 4 exciting tracks: AI/ML, Web Development, App Development, and Open Innovation.

What made H2C 1.0 special was its emphasis on mentorship and learning. We had industry experts from leading tech companies providing real-time guidance to participants. The event also featured technical workshops, networking sessions, and fun mini-events to keep the energy high throughout the hackathon.

The hackathon witnessed the creation of over 50 innovative projects, many of which addressed real-world challenges in healthcare, education, and sustainability. Several projects from H2C 1.0 have since evolved into successful startup ventures.
