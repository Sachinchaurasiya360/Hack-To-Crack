About H2C 2.0

Hack to Crack 2.0 marked our second edition, bringing together brilliant minds from across the nation. The event focused on fostering innovation and creating real-world solutions through technology.

Over 400+ participants from various colleges and universities came together for this 36-hour coding marathon. The event was held at vishwaniketan iMEET,

The hackathon witnessed the creation of over 100+ innovative projects, many of which addressed real-world challenges in healthcare, education, and sustainability.
